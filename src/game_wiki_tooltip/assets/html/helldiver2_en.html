Core Progression Systems

1. Warbonds: The Battle Pass That Never Expires

00:34

"Warbonds" are the game's version of a battle pass, but with a key difference: they never expire. You can unlock content at your own pace without fear of missing out.

  • Core Mechanic: Use Medals earned from completing missions and orders to unlock items.
  • Unlockable Content: Weapons, Armor, Helmets, Capes, Emotes, Victory Poses, and Super Credits.
  • Free Pass: The game includes the "Helldivers Mobilize!" free Warbond, providing plenty of starting gear.

2. Superstore & Super Credits

02:06

A rotating daily shop where you can purchase unique armor sets using "Super Credits".

  • Acquiring Currency:
    • Purchase with real money.
    • Found for free by exploring Points of Interest in missions.
    • Unlocked from Warbonds.

New Player Advice:

Prioritize using freely earned credits to unlock Premium Warbonds rather than spending in the store. You can absolutely unlock everything without spending any money.

Pre-Mission Preparation

3. Ship Management: Unlocks & Upgrades

03:12

At the Ship Management terminal, you can spend two types of resources to strengthen yourself.

Stratagems

Airstrikes, weapons, and other support called in during missions.

Unlock Currency: Requisition Slips (R-points)

Ship Modules

Provides permanent passive buffs for your Stratagems.

Unlock Currency: Common Samples Rare Samples Super Samples

Samples must be successfully extracted to be kept. Plan your upgrade path carefully.

4. Armory: Customizing Your Loadout

07:07
  • Body Armor: Crucial! Provides stats for Armor, Speed, and Stamina, and comes with a unique passive ability.
  • Helmets & Capes: Purely cosmetic items that offer no gameplay advantages.
  • Booster: Provides a team-wide passive buff for the mission, but the same effect does not stack.

5. Galactic War Map & Missions

09:55
  • Increased Difficulty: It doesn't just increase enemy health; it introduces new, stronger enemy types and new mission objectives.
  • Operation: Completing all missions in an Operation rewards bonus Medals.

6. Core Combat Mechanics

12:23

Weapon Customization

Hold the reload key (R) to adjust fire mode, scope magnification, and flashlight.

Realistic Reloading

Reloading discards the entire magazine and any remaining bullets. Reload wisely!

Physical Aiming

After a quick turn, you must wait for the gun to align with the crosshair for accurate shots.

7. Map Exploration & Hidden Mechanics

19:03
  • Minor Points of Interest (POIs): Explore unmarked locations on the map to find valuable resources like Medals, Super Credits, and Samples.
  • Radar Tower: Activating it will reveal all POIs on the map.
  • Raise Flag Objective: Using the "Salute" emote on the flagpole while it's raising will make it go faster!
